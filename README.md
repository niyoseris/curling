# Curling Mobile - Olympic Ice Challenge

Curling Mobile is a physics-based sports game that brings the strategy and precision of Olympic Curling to your mobile device. It features intuitive swipe controls and an advanced AI opponent for a realistic curling experience.

## ğŸš€ Features

- **Realistic Physics Engine:** Stone friction on ice, collisions between stones, and curl mechanics are entirely physics-based.
- **Intuitive Controls:** Use the dedicated "Swipe Zone" at the bottom of the screen to precisely adjust throw power and angle with your finger.
- **Smart AI Opponent:** Compete against a computer opponent that thinks strategically, attempting to land in the center or knock your stones out.
- **Score Tracking:** Play matches over 4 "Ends" (rounds) and track end-by-end and total scores on a detailed scoreboard.
- **Polished UI:** Ice blue theme, smooth animations, and haptic feedback for a high-quality user experience.

## ğŸ® How to Play

1. **Throwing:** Swipe your finger upward within the dark zone at the bottom of the screen.
2. **Power:** The speed and length of your swipe determine the throw's power.
3. **Aiming:** The angle of your swipe determines the stone's trajectory.
4. **Strategy:** Earn points by getting your stones closer to the center (house) than your opponent.
